About the orchestra

The Be'er Sheva Accordion Orchestra is a unique and exceptional phenomenon in Israeli music. Founded in 1982 by maestro Victor Derenboim, the orchestra consists of about 35 talented musicians with diverse backgrounds, ranging in age from 14 to 80. Their repertoire includes classical, folklore, jazz, and popular music. The orchestra rehearses once a week for two hours and performs twice a year in Beer Sheva, as well as at various festivals in Israel and abroad. Don't miss the opportunity to hear this amazing orchestra!
